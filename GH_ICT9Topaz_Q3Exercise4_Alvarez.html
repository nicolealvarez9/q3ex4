Athlete Description Net Worth

Nadia Comăneci		 Comăneci was once a Romanian gymnast who is known for being the first ever woman to score a perfect 10.0 in the Montreal 1976 Olympics. This was only the start of her making a name for herself as she then went on to achieve more. Her net worth is estimated to be $10 million.

Simone Biles		 Biles has earned herself a "superstar status" for winning gold medal after gold medal, only needing three more to gain the title of "USA's most decorated Olympic gymnast" (Bregman, 2019). Her net worth is said to be around $16 million.

Olga Korbut		 As a former Soviet Union gymnast, Korbut won a name for herself at 17 years old and captivated viewers during Munich 1972 Olympics with what is now called "The Korbut Flip". Most estimate her net worth to be $1 million - $5 million

Svetlana Khorkina		 She was a Russian gymnast known for changing gymnastics history with her unique and successful style of routines and moves. Khorkina's net worth could be ranging from $5 million - $10 million dollars

Nastia Liukin		 Liukin is a former American gymnast and child prodigy at the sport. She had some of the highest scores and ranks during her time and made Olympic history in the Beijin 2008 Games. She has a net worth of around $3 million.
